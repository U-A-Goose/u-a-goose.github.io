Welcome to Goose

We are the U-a-Goose (GrOup Of Software Engineering) group at University of Alberta, working at the intersection of:

  • Foundation Models for Software — building strong foundation models that can understand and reason about complex software systems at scale
  • SE4AI — applying and evaluating AI techniques within software engineering workflows

We analyze software artefacts — code, execution traces, bug reports, Q&A posts, and developer networks — to build automated tools that improve system reliability, developer productivity, and security.
